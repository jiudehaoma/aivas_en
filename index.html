About AIVAS APP

Traditional coaxial calibration relies on manual experience, taking 3-5 minutes, and is prone to problems such as poor edge cutting quality, inconsistent edges, and bottom slag due to human errors. The new AIVAS visual calibrator from Guangqi, relying on AI vision technology + high-precision circle fitting algorithm, reshapes the calibration process to achieve a dual breakthrough in accuracy and efficiency for you.

Functional Advantages

  • AI Intelligent Analysis: High-resolution visual sensor and AI intelligent algorithm, calibration accuracy <0.08mm, AI visual recognition, one-step manual debugging
